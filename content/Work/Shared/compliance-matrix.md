# Compliance Matrix

> Regulatory requirements matrix across both organizations.
> Every contract must clear the relevant gates. Updated when regulations change.

---

## By Organization

| Regulation | WFC | LTC | Requirement | Risk if Violated |
|------------|:---:|:---:|-------------|-----------------|
| FERPA | — | ✅ | Student data privacy; "school official" designation | Loss of district contracts; federal penalties |
| AB 1584 (CA Ed Code 49073.1) | — | ✅ | Pupil data contract provisions; breach notification | Invalidation of contract; liability |
| CALPADS | — | ✅ | CDE data submission standards compliance | District audit flags |
| HIPAA | ⚠️ | — | Not a covered entity, but therapist clients may be; avoid handling PHI | Indirect exposure through client relationships |
| CCPA / CPRA | ✅ | ✅ | California consumer privacy; data collection disclosure | Fines; client trust |
| CAN-SPAM | ✅ | — | Commercial email compliance for outreach | Low risk; but outreach must include opt-out |
| WCAG AA | ✅ | — | Accessibility standard for delivered websites | Client liability; ethical obligation |
| Professional Licensing (CA BBS) | ✅ | — | Therapists must maintain active license to claim credentials in marketing | Client reputational risk |
| CIS Controls v8 | — | ✅ | Security baseline for district data handling | District security audit failure |

## Contract Gate Checklist

Before any contract is sent, verify:

### WFC Contracts
- [ ] Pricing matches [[Work/Shared/pricing-catalog\\|Pricing Catalog]]
- [ ] No PHI handling commitment (HIPAA exposure prevention)
- [ ] Privacy policy referenced
- [ ] WCAG AA commitment stated
- [ ] Cancellation/termination terms clear

### LTC Contracts
- [ ] FERPA "school official" language present
- [ ] AB 1584 pupil data provisions included
- [ ] Data breach notification timeline (24 hours)
- [ ] Student PII handling restrictions stated
- [ ] PO requirement stated
- [ ] Insurance/CIS Controls referenced

## Notes
- ⚠️ HIPAA: WFC does not handle PHI. Therapist clients are responsible for their own HIPAA compliance. Do not agree to BAAs (Business Associate Agreements).
- FERPA and AB 1584 are non-negotiable in LTC contracts — these are statutory requirements, not optional terms.
- This matrix is operational guidance, not legal advice. Review with counsel.
