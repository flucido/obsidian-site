|---------|-------|-------|

### Pricing Factors
- District size (ADA): small (<5k) → mid (5–20k) → large (20k+)
- Data complexity: number of source systems, integration points
- Timeline urgency: standard vs. accelerated (25% premium)

## Cross-Org Pricing Rules

1. **No discount stacking** — only one discount/incentive per engagement
2. **Payment terms:** 50% upfront, 50% on delivery (WFC). Net-30 for LTC district contracts.
3. **Scope changes:** Priced at hourly rate ($150/hr WFC, $200/hr LTC) if outside original scope
4. **All prices subject to human review before quoting** — proposal template is a draft until approved
