# Social Media Outreach Campaign: Instagram + TikTok

## Pivot from Cold Outreach

This campaign replaces the email-based cold outreach model ([[email-campaign-marin-camft-launch|Launch Email Campaign]]) with a warmth-first social media strategy. No cold DMs. No cold emails. The goal is **inbound attraction** — therapists discover WellFull Collective through content that educates, empathizes, and demonstrates expertise.

---

## Campaign Identity

### Core Thesis (every piece of content traces to this)
> "Design should regulate before it converts."

### Audience (single target across both platforms)
| Attribute | Profile |
|-----------|---------|
| Role | Licensed therapist in private practice (LMFT, PsyD, LCSW) |
| Pain | Their website doesn't feel right but they can't articulate why |
| Current state | DIY template (Squarespace/Wix) they built years ago, feels generic |
| Deep need | To be taken seriously by visitors who are vulnerable and overwhelmed |
| Where they hang out | Instagram (personal/professional blend), TikTok (emerging for therapists) |

### Brand Visuals
| Token | Value | Usage |
|-------|-------|-------|
| Warm terracotta | `#ead5c8` | Feed backgrounds, story backgrounds |
| Sage green | `#dae8df` | Cards, pull-quote backgrounds |
| Espresso brown | `#5a3e2e` | Headline text, primary CTA elements |
| Dark slate | `#2e3d4a` | Body text |
| Rose gold / blush | `#c49088` | Accent highlights, decorative elements |
| Font — display | Cormorant Garamond | Headlines, pull quotes |
| Font — body | Atkinson Hyperlegible | Body copy, captions |

### Brand Voice
- Warm but not saccharine — this is clinical sensitivity, not wellness fluff
- Evidence-backed — reference research without being academic
- Non-urgent — no "act now," no countdowns, no FOMO
- Permission-giving — "If this resonates, we should talk" not "Book now or miss out"

### CTA Philosophy (from [[../studio/ctas|CTA Patterns]])
- Never urgency language
- Always show what happens after clicking
- Describe the user's action, not the business goal
- Secondary action always visible
- No dark patterns, no guilt-trip decline text

## Call-to-Action Strategy

### No direct sales CTAs on organic content
Instead, use a **soft landing** sequence:

1. **Educational content** → no CTA, just value
2. **Engagement content** → poll/question to build relationship
3. **Story bridge** → "If this resonated, check the link in bio"
4. **Landing page** → wellfullcollective.com → consult booking

### Link-in-bio destinations
| Destination | When to Promote |
|-------------|----------------|
| wellfullcollective.com | General brand awareness |
| wellfullcollective.com/consult | After educational content |
| Design audit (future) | Interactive tool: "How safe is your website?" |

### CTA Do Not Use (per studio rules)
- "Book now"
- "Limited spots available"
- "Act fast"
- Countdown timers
- Pre-checked consent boxes
- Guilt-trip decline text

### CTA Use Instead
- "If this resonates, we should talk"
- "Book a free consultation (no commitment, 15 minutes)"
- "Curious how this applies to your site?"
- "DM me your website for a no-obligation thought"

## Content Development Guidelines

### Visual Tone
- **Warm, not clinical** — use the brand palette's warm terracotta and sage tones
- **No stock photography of therapy sessions** — never simulate a therapy scene
- **Typography as visual** — let Cormorant Garamond headlines carry emotion
- **Generous white space** — mirrors the design principle of low cognitive load
- **No gradients** unless they match the warm terracotta-to-sage movement
- **No pure white backgrounds** (`#FFFFFF`) — use `#fcfaf6` or `#f2e4db` instead

### Caption Tone
- Write like a thoughtful colleague, not a brand
- Avoid marketing jargon: no "leverage," "optimize," "synergize"
- Lead with empathy: acknowledge the reader's reality before making a point
- End with a question or open loop, not a demand
- Keep paragraphs short (2–3 sentences max) — low cognitive load applies to copy too

### TikTok Delivery
- Frank addresses camera directly — authentic, slightly imperfect, not scripted
- Use natural lighting where possible — studio desk setup with warm lamp
- Closed captions always on (viewers watch without sound)
- Reference the research casually: "There's a study on this..." not "According to neuroaesthetic research P2/LPP timing..."
- Keep energy warm and conversational — this is about therapy, not tech

### Prohibited Content
- No identifiable client websites or logos without explicit permission
- No "before" images that could embarrass or expose a therapist
- No direct naming of competitors or platforms in a negative light (critique the category, not specific people)
- No clinical or therapeutic advice (we design for therapists, we are not therapists)
- No urgency or scarcity tactics (per CTA rules)
- No stock photos of people in therapy poses

## Governance

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Human approval | All posts must be reviewed before scheduling. No auto-publish without eyes. |
| Consistency | Brand colors and fonts must match the design token file. No ad-hoc deviations. |
| Anonymization | Any real site critique must be fully anonymized — no identifiable screenshots. |
| Engagement response | Respond to comments within 24 hours. Warm, personal, not templated. |
| Crisis protocol | If negative attention arises, pause posting and consult before responding. |
| Platform terms | Follow Instagram and TikTok community guidelines. No deceptive tactics. |
| Content library | Every piece of content links back to a vault artifact or research finding. |
