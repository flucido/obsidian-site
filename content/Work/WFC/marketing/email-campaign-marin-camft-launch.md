# Launch Email Campaign: Marin CAMFT Therapist Outreach

## Campaign Overview

- **Target List:** Marin CAMFT priority leads (20 therapists, scored 11–15)
- **Source:** Marin CAMFT directory → competitive analysis → priority scoring
- **Audience:** Licensed therapists (LMFT, PsyD, LCSW) in Marin County with independent websites
- **Cadence:** Single initial send + follow-up after 7 days (no response)
- **Goal:** Consult request → qualified lead → proposal
- **Status:** DRAFT — All sends require human approval

---

## Email 1: Initial Outreach (Day 0)

**Subject:** Your website and the visitor who arrives already overwhelmed

**Body:**

Hi {{first_name}},

I came across your practice through the Marin CAMFT directory and spent some time on your site at {{website}}.

The reason I'm reaching out is specific: my studio, WellFull Collective, builds websites for therapists — but we design for the visitor who arrives anxious, overwhelmed, or depleted. Not the hypothetical "average user" most web design targets.

Here is what we found after auditing 311 therapist websites across Marin County:

- 83% are built on DIY platforms (Squarespace, Wix, TherapySites)
- Most use templates that were designed for e-commerce or general small business — not for the emotional state of someone seeking therapy
- Very few have any evidence of professional design input

The gap is not about aesthetics. It is about cognitive safety. A visitor who is already activated does not need urgency cues, dense information, or aggressive CTAs. They need a digital environment that regulates before it converts.

We built WellFull Collective around this thesis. Our studio pairs clinical sensitivity (Dr. Lauren Ogren, PsyD, MFT #83783 — a licensed therapist who shapes positioning and trust language) with technical rigor (my side — 20+ years building performant, privacy-conscious web infrastructure).

No pitch decks here. If you are curious whether your site is doing what you think it is for the visitors who need it most, I am happy to offer a brief, no-obligation review. No strings — just seven minutes of honest feedback.

You can also see how we work at wellfullcollective.com.

Best,

Frank Lucido
Technical Implementation Lead, WellFull Collective
wellfullcollective.com

## Send Rules (Governance)

1. **Human approval required** before any send — hard stop
2. **Verify each recipient's email** is still active before sending
3. **Personalize** — replace {{first_name}} and {{website}} per recipient
4. **Track responses** in WFC pipeline as new consult requests
5. **Respect opt-out** immediately — no follow-up after unsubscribe or "not interested"
6. **Send during business hours** — Tuesday–Thursday, 10am–2pm PT
7. **Max 5 sends per day** to avoid spam filtering (batch over 4 days for 20 leads)
