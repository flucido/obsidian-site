## About Section

The people who visit therapy websites are not casually browsing. They may be anxious, overwhelmed, or depleted — and standard web design fails them. Urgency cues. Aggressive CTAs. Information density. All of it assumes cognitive availability that does not exist.

Our thesis: **design should regulate before it converts.**

WellFull Collective builds therapist websites that calm the nervous system, clarify the path forward, and respect the user's need for control. Every decision traces to neuroaesthetic research — not trends, not intuition, not competitor copying.

We pair two forms of credibility no other studio in this space offers:

**Dr. Lauren Ogren** (PsyD, MFT #83783) — Therapeutic Brand Positioning Consultant. She shapes how the site reads and sounds so the right client feels understood before they ever book.

**Frank Lucido** — Technical Implementation Lead. 20 years building performant, privacy-conscious, maintainable web infrastructure across private consulting, nonprofits, and public institutions.

What clients walk away with:
- A live website that passes six evidence-backed critique gates
- A Design System Token File calibrated to their brand's emotional targets
- An Emotional Journey Map showing how each page supports the visitor's psychological state
- A signed Critique Report proving every design decision has research behind it

We serve therapists who want their website to feel like a safe room — a space a visitor can enter, orient themselves in, and decide what comes next.
