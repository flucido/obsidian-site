## Key Finding: DIY Dominance

**83% of Marin CAMFT therapists use DIY platforms** (Squarespace + Wix + Weebly + GoDaddy + TherapySites). These are template-driven, self-built sites with minimal customization.

Only ~13% of sites have any detectable professional designer/agency credit — and most of those are platform-level credits, not actual design agencies.

## Competitive Opportunity

### The Gap
- **364 therapists** have independent websites in Marin CAMFT
- **~305 of them (83%)** are on DIY platforms with no professional design
- **No single agency** owns even 2% of this market
- Brighter Vision is the closest thing to a competitor (4 sites = 1.3% share)
- WordPress version numbers in "designer" credits suggest unmaintained sites (v4.9.4 from 2018, v6.4.8 from 2023)

### WFC Positioning
1. **No dominant competitor** — the market is wide open
2. **Squarespace dominance (39%)** — WFC can position as "Squarespace enhancement" or "beyond Squarespace" for therapists who've outgrown templates
3. **WordPress fragmentation** — 80 self-hosted WordPress sites, many running outdated versions, no central agency managing them
4. **TherapySites/Brighter Vision** — therapist-specific but template-driven. WFC can differentiate on custom design quality
5. **Dated/unmaintained sites** — WordPress 4.9.4, Artisteer v4 (ancient theme builder), Weebly (discontinued-ish) all signal neglect

### Recommended Outreach Angle
"Your colleagues in Marin CAMFT are on Squarespace and Wix. You all look the same. We build therapist sites that actually differentiate — and we know this community because we've studied every website in the directory."
