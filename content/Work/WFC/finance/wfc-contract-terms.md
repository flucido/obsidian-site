# WFC Contract Terms

> Standard engagement terms for WellFull Collective.
> Human review and approval required before any contract is sent.
> These are DRAFT terms — not legal advice. Review with counsel before first use.

---

## Standard Terms

### 1. Parties
- **Provider:** WellFull Collective (Frank Lucido, Dr. Lauren Ogren)
- **Client:** [Therapist Name / Practice Name]

### 2. Scope
As defined in the accepted proposal dated [proposal date], attached as Exhibit A.

### 3. Deliverables
- Live website at agreed domain
- Design system token file (CSS custom properties)
- 30 days post-launch support (bug fixes, minor adjustments)

### 4. Timeline
- Build period: [4–6 weeks] from deposit receipt
- Launch date: targeted [date], subject to client content delivery

### 5. Payment
- 50% deposit ($[amount]) due on signing
- 50% balance ($[amount]) due on launch
- Monthly support ($[amount]/month) begins on launch date, invoiced on 1st of each month

### 6. Client Responsibilities
- Provide brand assets (logo, photos, bios) within 1 week of deposit
- Provide written content or participate in content strategy session
- Review deliverables within 3 business days
- Delays >5 business days may shift timeline

### 7. Intellectual Property
- Client owns the website and all content upon full payment
- Provider retains right to display work in portfolio unless client opts out in writing
- Design system tokens are co-owned — client may use; provider may reuse methodology

### 8. Termination
- Either party may terminate with 14 days written notice
- If terminated before launch: client pays for work completed at $150/hr, minus deposit
- If terminated after launch: support retainer ends at month-end

### 9. Limitation of Liability
- Provider liability limited to fees paid
- Provider not liable for third-party service disruptions (hosting, email delivery)

### 10. Governing Law
- California

## Anti-Patterns
