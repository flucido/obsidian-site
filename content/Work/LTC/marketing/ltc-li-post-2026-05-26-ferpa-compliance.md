# FERPA Compliance Does Not Require a $50K Software Contract

**Hook:** One of the most expensive myths in K-12 technology is that student data compliance requires an expensive black-box platform.

---

One of the most expensive myths in K-12 technology is that student data compliance requires an expensive black-box platform.

It usually does not.

What districts need is not a glossy dashboard.
They need a defensible process.

The difference I see most often:

The expensive route gives districts reports they can download.
The better route gives districts evidence they can explain.

When an auditor or administrator asks, "How do you know this is controlled?"
the strongest answer is not, "Our vendor says so."

The strongest answer is:
"Here is the access model."
"Here are the logs."
"Here is the workflow."
"Here is the documentation."

Compliance maturity is less about buying certainty and more about building transparency.
