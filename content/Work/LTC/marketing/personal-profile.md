# Frank Lucido — Professional Profile

## Core Narrative

Most school districts are building a "Data Debt" that will compromise student privacy by 2030.

After 15 years leading technology operations for global educational institutions like Room to Read and Making Waves Foundation, I've witnessed how fragmented data infrastructure kills mission impact. The chaos isn't just inefficiency — it's a compliance time bomb.

## The Problem with Cloud-Only AI

School districts face an impossible paradox: You need AI to manage overwhelming administrative burdens (scheduling, grading, state reporting), but cloud-based tools create "data egress" vulnerabilities that terrify boards and violate California privacy laws like AB 1584 and SOPPA.

## The Local-First Solution

We architect AI systems that keep student data on YOUR infrastructure:

→ Sub-50ms inference (no cloud round-trips)
→ Zero PII leaving facility network
→ Fixed infrastructure costs vs. variable token fees
→ Full FERPA/GDPR compliance by design

## Proven Results
