# LTC Contract Terms

> Standard engagement terms for Lucido Technology Consulting.
> Human review and approval required before any contract is sent.
> These are DRAFT terms — not legal advice. Review with counsel before first use.

---

## Standard Terms

### 1. Parties
- **Provider:** Lucido Technology Consulting (Frank Lucido)
- **Client:** [District Name], [County], California

### 2. Scope
As defined in the accepted proposal dated [proposal date], attached as Exhibit A.

### 3. Compliance (Mandatory)
- **FERPA:** Provider is designated as a "school official" with legitimate educational interest. All student data handled per district data sharing agreement.
- **AB 1584 (CA Ed Code 49073.1):** Contract includes required provisions for pupil data protection, prohibition on targeted advertising, and data breach response.
- **CALPADS:** All data handling complies with CDE CALPADS data submission standards.
- **CIS Controls:** Provider maintains security practices aligned with CIS Critical Security Controls v8.

### 4. Deliverables
Defined per proposal Exhibit A. Standard deliverables include:
- Technical documentation (architecture, data flows, runbooks)
- Working system or integration
- Staff training materials and sessions
- Post-launch support period (30–90 days)

### 5. Timeline
- Start: upon receipt of signed contract + PO
- Milestone dates per Exhibit A
- Delays caused by district (access, approvals) extend timeline accordingly

### 6. Payment (Net-30)
| Milestone | Amount | Trigger |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| Start | 30% | Signed contract + PO |
| Mid-point | 40% | Milestone deliverable accepted |
| Completion | 30% | Final delivery accepted |

### 7. District Responsibilities
- Provide timely system access and stakeholder availability
- Designate single point of contact for approvals
- Review deliverables within 10 business days
- Issue PO within district procurement timeline

### 8. Intellectual Property
- District owns all deliverables, documentation, and custom code upon full payment
- Provider retains right to use anonymized methodologies and non-identifying process documentation
- Open source components used where applicable (licensed per their terms)

### 9. Data Handling
- All student PII processed on district-approved systems only
- No student data stored on provider devices beyond engagement period
- Data breach: provider notifies district within 24 hours of discovery

### 10. Termination
- Either party may terminate with 30 days written notice
- District pays for work completed through termination date
- All district data returned or destroyed within 14 days of termination

### 11. Insurance
- Provider maintains professional liability insurance
- Certificate of Insurance provided upon request

### 12. Governing Law
- California. Any disputes subject to [County] County jurisdiction.

---

## Anti-Patterns
- Do NOT remove or weaken FERPA/AB 1584 language — these are non-negotiable
- Do NOT begin work without signed contract + PO
- Do NOT store student PII locally — use district systems only
