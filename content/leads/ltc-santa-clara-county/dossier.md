## CARE Tool Context

> "CARE addresses this challenge by allowing districts to check their data earlier, using the same validation rules applied by the state, but presented in a clearer and more actionable way."

**LTC's wedge:** CARE identifies problems. LTC fixes them. Districts that just laid off staff don't have the capacity to resolve validation errors at scale. SCCOE's tool creates demand for implementation support.
