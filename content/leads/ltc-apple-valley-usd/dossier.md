### Key Findings Relevant to LTC Pitch

**Section 14 — Information Systems & Data Management: ALL YES ✅**
- This was the ONLY section with a perfect score. AVUSD uses integrated financial/HR system, same platform as COE (San Bernardino), CALPADS certified on time. This is their single bright spot in an otherwise damning report.

**Section 15 — Internal Controls & Fraud Prevention: MULTIPLE FAILS ❌**
- No anonymous fraud hotline, no internal audit function
- AR duties not segregated: same person prepares invoices AND receives payments
- Access rights not updated on employment changes
- These are control environment deficiencies that data migration governance directly addresses

**Other Red Flags:**
- Using one-time funds for ongoing staff salaries — no sustainment plan
- Collective bargaining: zero 2025-26 settlements, missing presettlement analyses
- Multiyear projections include $4.5M in unsubstantiated savings (not board-approved)
- $2.24M/year early retirement incentive (SRP) hitting unrestricted GF through 2029
- Facilities only 74% utilized — operational capacity issue
- Board members don't attend budget training; no formal budget training for administrators

### How FCMAT Shapes the LTC Pitch

**The Core Tension:** AVUSD is cutting $3.5M in 2026-27. Any new spending faces intense scrutiny. BUT the SIS migration is already funded (RFP approved before FCMAT, Focus contract signed). The data team is doing the work regardless.

**Revised Pitch Strategy — "Protect Section 14":**
The FCMAT report gives the IT department its only clean score. An Aeries→Focus migration puts that at risk. One missed CALPADS certification or data integrity failure turns a clean section into a finding — exactly when the board and county office are scrutinizing everything.

**The Internal Controls Hook:** Section 15 failings create an opening. Data migration governance (validation rules, reconciliation protocols, audit trails) IS an internal control. LTC can position as directly addressing FCMAT findings, not creating new costs.

**Budget Sensitivity Mitigation:**
- Frame as migration risk insurance, not new spending
- Cost of a failed CALPADS cycle or data corruption event >> QA engagement cost
- Position as short-term, project-based (not ongoing overhead)
- Could be scoped as part of the existing Focus implementation budget
