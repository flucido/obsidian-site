# Education Vertical — Market Research

> Target market intelligence for Lucido Technology Consulting.
> Informs pipeline targeting, pricing, and competitive positioning.

---

## Market Sizing

| Segment | CA Entities | LTC Addressable | Notes |
|---------|------------|-----------------|-------|
| K-12 School Districts | ~1,000 | ~300 (mid-size, 5–20k ADA) | Small districts lack budget; large ones have in-house teams |
| County Offices of Education (COE) | 58 | ~15 (data-active COEs) | SCCOE, LACOE, etc. — regional data hubs |
| Charter School Networks | ~1,300 | ~50 (networks with 3+ schools) | Often use separate SIS from host district |

## Demand Signals

| Signal | Strength | Example |
|--------|----------|---------|
| Active SIS migration | VERY HIGH | Apple Valley USD (Aeries → Focus, Aug 2026) |
| CALPADS submission errors | HIGH | Districts flagged in CDE CALPADS flash reports |
| LCAP data management initiatives | MEDIUM | Beverly Hills USD FY25-26 budget line item |
| COE regional data tools launch | MEDIUM | SCCOE CARE tool (April 2026) |
| Superintendent / CTO turnover | LOW-MEDIUM | New leadership = openness to change |

---

## Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | Type | Threat |
|------------|------|--------|
| County Office of Education (COE) | Regional service provider | HIGH — districts default to COE for data help |
| Ed-Fi Alliance implementers | Specialized SIS integrators | MEDIUM |
| General IT consultancies (Deloitte, Accenture) | Enterprise | LOW (too expensive for mid-size districts) |
| In-house district IT | Internal | HIGH — many districts build internally |

### LTC Differentiation
- DuckDB/Polars stack — lightweight, no enterprise licensing
- CALPADS expertise — specific to CA compliance
- Affordable for mid-size districts (most consultancies ignore <$25K deals)
- Open-source tools reduce vendor lock-in

## Lead Discovery Sources

| Source | Signal Quality | Actionability | Best Use |
|--------|---------------|---------------|----------|
| CA Dept of Ed SIS vendor list | HIGH | HIGH | Identify districts on legacy SIS |
| District Board Meeting Minutes | HIGH | MEDIUM (manual) | Catch procurement discussions |
| EdWeek / EdSource | MEDIUM | LOW | Industry context, not leads |
| CALPADS Flash Issues Reports | HIGH | HIGH | Identify districts with data quality problems |
| District RFPs (public portals) | HIGH | HIGH | Direct procurement opportunities |
| LCAP documents | MEDIUM | MEDIUM | Data management initiatives |

---

## Recommended Targeting Strategy
1. **Primary:** Districts with active SIS migrations (public RFPs, board minutes, vendor change announcements)
2. **Secondary:** CALPADS-flagged districts — data quality problems create urgency
3. **Tertiary:** COE partnerships — leverage county-level relationships to reach multiple districts
