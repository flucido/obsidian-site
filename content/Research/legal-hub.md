## Partnership Agreement — POPULATED

[[Research/partnership-agreement|Partnership Agreement]] — Proposed terms:

### WFC Internal (Lucido + Ogren)
- **Revenue split:** 50/50 for core services (WFC-01/02/03). Weighted for standalone: Brand Positioning 30/70 (Ogren-led), Tech Support 80/20 (Lucido-led).
- **Decision rights:** Mutual for pricing, client acceptance, outbound sends. Ogren leads brand/clinical. Lucido leads technical/operations.
- **Time commitment:** Ogren ~5–10 hrs/week. Lucido variable (project-based, balances LTC).
- **Expenses:** WFC operating expenses from WFC account. Shared infra (Hermes, Obsidian) borne by Frank/LTC.
- **Dissolution:** 90-day wind-down. NeroDesign reverts to Frank. Clients choose continuation partner.

### Cross-Entity (WFC ↔ LTC)
- **Referrals:** No fee — Frank owns both.
- **Shared resources:** Obsidian vault + Hermes infra provided by LTC, used by both.
- **NeroDesign:** Frank-owned, WFC-licensed royalty-free.
- **No conflicting positioning:** K-12 consulting and therapy websites don't compete.

**Open:** Partner discussion needed on revenue split, time commitment, expense allocation, NeroDesign IP transfer if WFC grows.
