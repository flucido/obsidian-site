## Competitive Landscape

### Direct Competitors (Therapy Website Agencies)
| Competitor | Starting Price | Differentiator | Threat Level |
|------------|---------------|----------------|-------------|
| TherapistX | $3,500+ | Market leader, strong SEO | HIGH |
| Brighter Vision | $59–99/mo (subscription) | Low monthly cost, no upfront | MEDIUM |
| TherapySites | Included with directory listing | Bundled with directory | LOW (low quality) |

### WFC Differentiation
- $1,500 entry point undercuts TherapistX
- Licensed clinician on team (Dr. Ogren) — no competitor has this
- Nervous-system-aware design — unique positioning
- Not a subscription trap — client owns the site

### Key Finding from Marin CAMFT
- **83% of therapists use DIY platforms** (Squarespace, Wix, Weebly)
- **No single agency dominates** any CAMFT chapter — market is fragmented
- Most therapist websites are template-driven, undifferentiated, and don't reflect clinical expertise
- Platform migration is the primary opportunity, not greenfield builds
