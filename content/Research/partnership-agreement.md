## Part 1: WFC Internal Partnership (Lucido + Ogren)

### 1.1 Roles and Responsibilities

| Partner | Role | Primary Responsibilities |
|---------|------|-------------------------|
| **Frank Lucido** | Technical + Operations Director | Website design and development, hosting infrastructure, tooling (Hermes, Obsidian, Turso), pipeline operations, financial management, pricing, contracting, invoicing |
| **Dr. Lauren Ogren** | Clinical + Brand Director | Therapeutic brand positioning, client consultation and discovery, clinical credibility and network, therapist community outreach, brand voice and messaging, content direction |

### 1.2 Revenue Split

| Tier | Lucido | Ogren | Rationale |
|------|--------|-------|-----------|
| WFC-01 Landing Page ($1,500) | 50% ($750) | 50% ($750) | Equal split — both contribute essential work |
| WFC-02 CMS Site ($3,500) | 50% ($1,750) | 50% ($1,750) | Equal split |
| WFC-03 Web Application (custom) | 50% | 50% | Equal split |
| Monthly Support Retainer ($100–250) | 50% | 50% | Ongoing maintenance + client relationship |
| Standalone Brand Positioning ($750) | 30% ($225) | 70% ($525) | Ogren-led service |
| Standalone Tech Support ($500) | 80% ($400) | 20% ($100) | Lucido-led service |

**Distribution:** Revenue deposited to WFC business account. Member draws distributed monthly per split above after expense allocation.

### 1.3 Decision Rights

| Decision | Authority | Notes |
|----------|-----------|-------|
| Pricing changes | Mutual | Both must agree |
| Client acceptance | Mutual | Either can veto a client fit concern |
| Outbound sends | Mutual | Both must approve before any outreach goes live |
| Brand and positioning | Ogren leads | Lucido consult |
| Technical architecture | Lucido leads | Ogren informed |
| Website design direction | Ogren leads (clinical eye) | Lucido implements |
| Financial decisions >$1,000 | Mutual | Under $1,000: either can decide |
| Hiring contractors | Mutual | For WFC-specific work |
| Partnership changes | Mutual | Revenue split, role changes, new partners |

### 1.4 Time Commitment

| Partner | Expected Commitment | Notes |
|---------|--------------------|-------|
| Frank Lucido | Variable (project-based) | Balances LTC + WFC. Depth varies by active build cycle. |
| Dr. Lauren Ogren | ~5–10 hrs/week | Client consults, brand positioning sessions, network building |

Compensation reflects value contributed, not hours logged. Both partners bring non-substitutable expertise.

### 1.5 Expenses

**WFC Operating Expenses** (paid from WFC account before profit distribution):
- Hosting (Vercel): ~$20/month
- Domain (wellfullcollective.com): ~$15/year
- Resend (email): free tier → paid as volume grows
- Tools and software specific to WFC

**Shared Infrastructure** (borne by Frank/LTC — not WFC expense):
- Hermes Agent infrastructure
- Obsidian vault hosting
- Turso database

### 1.6 Dissolution

If the partnership ends:
- WFC entity assets (codebase, brand, domain, client list) sold or divided per ownership
- Outstanding contracts completed or transferred
- Client relationships: clients choose to continue with either partner
- NeroDesign methodology: reverts to Frank (license terminates with WFC)
- Brand name "WellFull Collective": both partners retain non-exclusive reference rights for portfolio
- 90-day wind-down period for active engagements

## Part 2: Cross-Entity Terms (WFC ↔ LTC)

### 2.1 Shared Resources

| Resource | Provided By | Cost Bearer | Terms |
|----------|------------|-------------|-------|
| Obsidian vault (operational state) | Frank Lucido | LTC bears infra cost | Both orgs have full operational access |
| Hermes Agent swarm | Frank Lucido | LTC bears compute cost | Both orgs use for pipeline, ops, research |
| NeroDesign methodology | Frank Lucido | — | WFC licensed royalty-free, perpetual. LTC may reference methodology. |
| frank-lucido-site | LTC | LTC | WFC referenced/cross-linked, not hosted |
| wellfullcollective site | WFC | WFC | LTC may cross-link from frank-lucido-site |

### 2.2 Client Referrals

| Direction | Example | Handling |
|-----------|---------|----------|
| LTC → WFC | District superintendent needs therapist website | Warm intro to WFC. No referral fee (Frank owns both). |
| WFC → LTC | Therapist's spouse is district CTO | Warm intro to LTC. No referral fee. |
| External → either | Someone finds Frank through LinkedIn | Route to correct entity based on need. |

**Rule:** Referrals between entities carry no fee since Frank Lucido is the common owner. The benefit is mutual pipeline growth.

### 2.3 Brand and Market Positioning

- **LTC** positions Frank as K-12 data infrastructure expert
- **WFC** positions Frank + Lauren as therapist website specialists
- **LinkedIn content** (LTC-owned) builds Frank's authority — benefits both entities
- **NeroDesign** methodology positions WFC as evidence-driven — differentiates from competitors
- No conflicting positioning: K-12 consulting and therapy websites don't compete

### 2.4 Conflict Resolution

If cross-entity conflicts arise (resource allocation, time priority, client overlap):
1. Discuss directly (Lucido + Ogren for WFC matters; Lucido solo for LTC matters)
2. Priority by client deadline and revenue impact
3. WFC active builds take priority over LTC pipeline development (LTC is longer sales cycle)
4. Neither entity blocks the other's legitimate business
